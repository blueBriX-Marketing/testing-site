Connect your HubSpot account

This dashboard syncs directly with HubSpot. You need a Private App token — free to create, stored only in your browser.

  1. Go to HubSpot → Settings → Integrations → Private Apps
  2. Click "Create a private app" → name it blueBrix Dashboard
  3. Under Scopes, tick: crm.objects.deals.read, crm.objects.deals.write, crm.objects.contacts.read, crm.schemas.deals.write
  4. Click "Create app" → copy the token (starts with pat-na1-)

⚠ Stored in localStorage only. Never sent anywhere except directly to api.hubapi.com. Do not use on a shared computer.
